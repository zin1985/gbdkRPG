RPG319 TITLE PROLOGUE SCROLL NOTE

User request:
- When selecting はじめから, play a prologue before starting the game.
- Text should rise slowly from the bottom, fade in, scroll upward, then fade out and disappear.
- Holding A speeds up the scroll.
- START opens a skip prompt: スキップしますか？ はい / いいえ.
- The prologue should describe the hidden setting only as a vague legend.

Implementation:
- Added a banked prologue player inside save_runtime.c so Bank 0 impact is minimal.
- save_runtime_title_load() now calls save_runtime_play_prologue() before returning SAVE_TITLE_NEW.
- Prologue text is split into 10 short Japanese lines.
- CGB row attributes use multiple BG palettes to simulate fade-in/fade-out by row: dim at top/bottom and normal near the center.
- A held during scroll reduces frame delay.
- START opens a framed confirmation prompt; A confirms, B/START cancels.

Prologue text:
はるかな地より
魔王はあらわれた。
獣も人も姿を変えられ
古き都は灰となった。
その力の源を
知る者はいない。
残された者は
奪った秘術を魔法と呼び
闇の支配に抗う火を
ひそかに守っている。
