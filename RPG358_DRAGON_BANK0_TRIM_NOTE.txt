rpg358_red_dragon_bank0_trim

Purpose:
- Fix Bank 0 overflow introduced by rpg356/rpg357 red dragon flame animation.

Change:
- Removed the Bank0-side per-frame battle_tick_enemy_animation() helper and MODE_BATTLE loop call.
- Dragon body remains BG 144x48 via banked sprites.c.
- Dragon flame remains OBJ and is shown as the initial flame frame during enemy redraw.

Reason:
- safe build showed Bank0 overflow around 0x4000-0x4077.
- The helper built alive_flags in main.c every battle loop and pushed Bank0 over the limit.

Next step:
- Reintroduce true flame animation later via a banked runtime module or an existing banked frame hook, not by adding loops to main.c.
