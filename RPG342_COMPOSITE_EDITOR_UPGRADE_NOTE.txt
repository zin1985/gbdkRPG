OBJ management note:
- GB/GBC hardware max is 40 OBJ total, with a 10 OBJ per scanline limit.
- This project currently reserves OAM 31-38 for enemy overlay pieces.
- Therefore the practical safe maximum for the composite enemy editor is 8 overlay OBJ.
- Each editor OBJ is handled as 8x16, consuming two 8x8 tiles.
