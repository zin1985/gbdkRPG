RPG351 FORCE PLANT PAIR CHEST NOTE

Issue:
- Field debug chest still showed only プラントアイ x1.
- The previous implementation relied on seed=0 + area=7 selecting encounter 8 through the cave random branch.
- In practice this was too fragile when encounter indexes changed or an older table was mixed in.

Fix:
- Added special area id 14 in battle_data_runtime.c.
- area 14 directly forces encounter_id = 8.
- Field plant debug chest now calls battle_data_load_random_area(0u, 14u, ...).
- encounter 8 is the intended pair: プラントアイ + プラントゲイズ.

Files changed:
- battle_data_runtime.c
- main.c
- Makefile
