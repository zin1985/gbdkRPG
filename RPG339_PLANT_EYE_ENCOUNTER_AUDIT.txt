Runtime area selection in battle_data_runtime.c:
- area == 12: encounter 13 = ミミック
- area == 13: encounter 12 = どうくつのぬし
- area >= 7 && area <= 10: encounter 8 or 9 = プラントアイ included 100%
- area == 6: encounter 2 or 3 = けもの/ウォーマシン
- other random areas: encounter seed % 8 = 0..7, so プラントアイ x1 can now appear via encounter 7

Important fix in RPG339:
- Previously プラントアイ existed only in encounter 8/9, which are selected only in AREA_CAVE_1..4.
- RPG339 also places プラントアイ x1 in encounter 7.
- This means normal random areas can also confirm プラントアイ without changing NONBANKED battle_data_runtime.c.
- This avoids increasing Bank 0.
