RPG312 MIMIC COLORFUL LOOT NOTE

User request:
- Based on the reference image, make the scattered lower-right loot on the mimic colorful: red, blue, green, yellow.

What changed:
- Added special M-size draw handling for sprite_kind 6 (mimic) in sprites.c.
- Instead of filling the entire 4x4 enemy area with one palette, mimic now writes a per-tile attribute map.
- Most tiles still use the mimic body/chest palette.
- The lower-right 2x2 tiles are recolored to create a colorful treasure area:
  - row2 col2: mimic red/brown jewel tone
  - row2 col3: blue jewel (palette 3)
  - row3 col2: green jewel (palette 1)
  - row3 col3: yellow coin/gold (palette 6)
- No palette definitions were changed in this step; this only changes which existing GBC palettes are used on the mimic's lower-right tiles.

Files changed:
- sprites.c
- Makefile
