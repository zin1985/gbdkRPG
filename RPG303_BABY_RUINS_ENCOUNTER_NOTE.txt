RPG303 BABY RUINS ENCOUNTER NOTE

Issue:
- RPG302 added ベビー enemy data and encounter_table entries, but battle_data_load_random_area() did not select those new encounter indices.
- Therefore ベビー was defined but not naturally reachable by random encounter.

Fix:
- Updated battle_data_runtime.c so AREA_RUINS (area == 4) selects encounter_id 10 or 11.
- encounter_id 10 = ベビー x1
- encounter_id 11 = ベビー + けもの

Result:
- ベビー can now be encountered in the ruins random encounter area.
- Cave and field encounter selection remain unchanged.
