Fixes Bank0 overflow caused by the previous battle item enemy-art restoration helper.
- Removed two static restore helper functions from main.c and the unconditional battle_enter_render_once() restore call.
- Added a smaller local restore loop only in player_battle_item() before rebuilding the battle screen.
- L-size tile loader no longer remaps sprite_kinds to 0; L drawing ignores sprite_kind anyway.
- This should reduce Bank0 usage while still preventing enemy art from changing after returning from the battle item menu.
- Inventory first-scroll and odd-last-row fixes remain preserved.
