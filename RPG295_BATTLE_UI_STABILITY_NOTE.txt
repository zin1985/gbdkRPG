rpg295 battle UI fixes

1) Top party icons
- battle_place_party_icons() now forces SPRITES_8x16 before placing the icons.
- Fix target: the upper party mini-sprites showing only their top half.

2) Top status layout
- The まほう block in battle_status_ui_runtime_draw_party_status_box() moved
  left by one tile (x=14 -> x=13).

3) Field player sprite leakage
- battle_hide_window_and_reset_scroll() now explicitly hides the field-player
  OBJ slots (PLAYER_SPRITE_BASE + 0..3) whenever battle restores its screen.

4) Top status text changing after battle item selection
- battle_update_dirty() now redraws the top status box and party icons on every
  battle UI refresh so shared JP glyph-cache churn from lower message/item text
  does not leave the top names/labels corrupted.
