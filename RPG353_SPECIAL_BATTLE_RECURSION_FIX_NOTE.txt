RPG353 SPECIAL BATTLE RECURSION FIX NOTE

Issue:
- RPG352 froze when starting the plant pair chest battle.

Cause:
- battle_copy_all_enemies_from_data() accidentally called itself recursively instead of looping over enemies.
- This caused infinite recursion / stack overflow at battle start.

Fix:
- battle_copy_all_enemies_from_data() now loops over battle_enemy_count and calls battle_copy_enemy_from_data(i).
- This preserves the intended all-enemy copy behavior for special battles.

Expected result:
- Field chest battle should start without freezing.
- The preloaded pair encounter should be copied into both enemy slots.

Files changed:
- main.c
- Makefile
