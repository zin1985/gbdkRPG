RPG321 PROLOGUE SMOOTHER FAST A NOTE

User request:
- The mood is good, but the prologue flickers.
- Make the scroll smoother.
- Make A-button speed-up faster.

What changed:
- save_runtime.c prologue rendering was changed to avoid redrawing the whole visible BG area every pixel.
- Text rows are now redrawn only when the tile-row position changes; per-frame 1-pixel movement is handled by move_bkg()/SCY.
- Instead of clearing all 20 visible rows every frame, only the old text rows are cleared and the new text rows are drawn on tile-boundary updates.
- Normal scroll remains smooth and slow: 1 px step with 3-frame wait.
- Holding A now advances faster: 2 px step with 1-frame wait.
- Returning from the START skip confirmation now rebuilds the prologue view cleanly at the current scroll position.

Files changed:
- save_runtime.c
- Makefile
